NYC highest rated all girls gym

Venus Vitality is NYC's largest all girls gym. This gym was founded by Aneicea Aaron back in 2024. Aaron wanted to provide the women in the community with a safe space to feel empowered, comfortable and relaxed. Venuss Vitality classes are a high octane full-body class experience. Our instuctors are hand-picked for their extensive fitness training and their remarkable dedication to wellness innovation. Every woman who wished to invest in their health is welcome. Whether you're a competitive athlete, or just getting started, we're here to guide you on your fitness journey.
